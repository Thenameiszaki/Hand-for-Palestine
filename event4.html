Mass Rally in Support of Palestinians

Malaysia has a longstanding history of supporting the Palestinian cause through mass rallies. On October 22, 2023, thousands gathered in Kuala Lumpur at Axiata Arena Stadium for the "Malaysia is with Palestine" rally. Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim reaffirmed Malaysia's unwavering support for Palestine.

From December 26-31, 2024, activists organized the "Blockade for Palestine" sit-in protest outside the U.S. Embassy, expressing frustration over the U.S. veto on ceasefire resolutions. Despite challenges, demonstrators showed resilience and solidarity.
